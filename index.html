About
Me

Thank you for visiting my website! My name is Sam Gates, and I am a Raleigh native who has had a passion for computers and programming from a very young age. I started by taking apart and upgrading my family's computer before I hit the age of 10. From there, I was a self-taught web developer in my teenage years. For a couple years, I had a web design and hosting company called Open Source Rules, LLC. I routinely worked with PHP, JavaScript, MySQL, CSS, and HTML. I had servers in my garage hosting websites that I configured using open source tools such as Apache, phpMyAdmin, cPanel, and FreeBSD. In high school, I took courses in Java and network engineering.

During college, I switched gears and pursued achieving fluency in the German language. I graduated Summa Cum Laude from NC State with a German Studies degree. Over time, I started working on websites less and less. I am thrilled to be pursuing my passion for computers and programming again! Please see below for my portfolio.
